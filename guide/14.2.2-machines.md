### 14.2.2. Machines


```
Machines are the remnants of the old world before the 'Great Collapse'.
They vary in their design and function, but all have the same purpose - to
destroy. They are immune to most standard status abnormalities, but may
suffer similar effects from other means. They are usually quite resistant
to some elemental damage, and this may stretch into immunity in some cases.
Most machines are encountered in hi-tech dungeons.
```
