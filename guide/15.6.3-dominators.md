### 15.6.3. Dominators


```
Met: Vahal Fort                 Recommended Level before fighting:
  HP: 999                          Party: 37
 EXP: 14400
 MST: 1500


Three Dominators are the sum total of Daughter's defences, and it shouldn't

be a great chore to defeat them. Use hit-all Techniques and Skills (try out
Wren's POSIBOLT), and the Dominators should only last a couple of rounds.
```
