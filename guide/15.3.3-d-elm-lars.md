### 15.3.3. D-Elm-Lars


```
Met: Climate Control Centre     Recommended Level before fighting:
  HP: 777                          Chaz: 27       Wren: 26
 EXP: 3919                         Rika: 28-29    Raja: 29
 MST: 500                          Rune: 26-27


This fellow is ridiculously easy. All you need to do in this battle is
have Chaz, Rika and Wren attack, and let Rune and Raja just defend. Even
doing this, the battle will only last a couple of rounds.
```
