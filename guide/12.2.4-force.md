### 12.2.4. Force


```
Force abilities make use of air and general physical forces to pummel and
slice targets. They can be in the form of powerful winds or physical
strikes.


Techniques: ZAN, GIZAN, NAZAN

Skills:     HEWN, PHONON, BURSTROC, CORRSION

Combos:     SilentWave

Weapons:    All except those mentioned elsewhere


Effective Against:   -

Ineffective Against: Slimes, Ghosts, Scorpions, Sentries, Flamers, Undead,
                     Servitors, Armour Mechs, Rip Fiends, De-Vars, Sa-Lews
```
