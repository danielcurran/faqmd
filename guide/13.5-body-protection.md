## 13.5. Body Protection


```
Body protection is a character's main line of defence against enemies'
physical attacks. It varies in size, shape and material, and is designed to
deflect or cushion blows to reduce damage to the wearer. There are a fair
few types of body protection, each suitable for the different types of
character. There are light outfits for physically weaker characters, and
heavier suits for stronger characters.

This section gives a list of each item of body protection in order of DFS
bonus, split into the four different types. The symbols following the head
protection names indicate which characters can wear them:

Ch:Chaz, Al:Alys, Ha:Hahn, Ru:Rune, Gr:Gryz, Ri:Rika,
De:Demi, Wr:Wren, Ra:Raja, Ky:Kyra, Se:Seth
---------------------------------------------
```
