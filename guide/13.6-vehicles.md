## 13.6. Vehicles


```
There are three vehicles in the game, each having a specific purpose at the
time it is acquired. The following sections describe each vehicle in
detail, including properties both in and out of battle. The statistics
given for each vehicle are very roughly based on the character statistics,
but are intended more as a means of comparison between each vehicle rather
than with the individual party members. I hope to give more accurate values
for these statistics at a later date.

The vehicles cannot be sold.
```
