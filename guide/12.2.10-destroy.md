### 12.2.10. Destroy


```
Destroy abilities are used to utterly annihilate creatures, and can work
against all but non-material or very tough beings.


Techniques: BROSE

Skills:     CRASH, EXPLODE, NEGATIS

Combos:     Black Hole

Weapons:    -


Effective Against:   All but Ghosts, Sandworms, Prophallus or bosses

Ineffective Against: Ghosts, Sandworms, Prophallus, all bosses
```
