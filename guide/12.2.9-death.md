### 12.2.9. Death


```
Death abilities are used to stop the natural process of life, and so only
work against creatures that could be regarded as organic.


Techniques: VOL, SAVOL

Skills:     DIEM, ELIMINAT, DEATH, DTHSPELL

Combos:     Holocaust, Lethal Img

Weapons:    SHADWBLADE, GENOCYCLAW


Effective Against:   All Biomonsters, Darkspawn & Evil Beings (not Ghosts,
                     Undead, Rip Fiends, Sandworms, Prophallus or bosses)

Ineffective Against: Machines, Ghosts, Undead, Rip Fiends, Sandworms,
                     Prophallus, all bosses
```
