### 12.2.6. Electric


```
Electric abilities make use of lightning and powerful electrical currents
to cause shocking damage to targets. They are in the form of bolts of high-
intensity electrical charge.


Techniques: -

Skills:     TANDLE

Combos:     CondctThnd

Weapons:    THUNDRCLAW


Effective Against:   All Machines, Slimes, Frogs, Lizard-Men

Ineffective Against: Scorpions, all Blades, Sabers, Ghosts, Rip Fiends
```
