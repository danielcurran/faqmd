### 14.2.5. Unique


```
Unique enemies are very few in number, and are one-of-a-kind encounters
that are usually bosses. They do not readily fall into any of the above
categories, and are therefore varied in all ways.
Unique            - Dark Force  (1-Boss)
                    Dark Force  (2-Boss)
                    Dark Force  (3-Boss)
                    Carnivorous Trees (Boss)
                    De-Vars     (Boss)
                    Sa-Lews     (Boss)
                    Re-Faze     (Boss)
                    Blue Rappy
                    Rappy
                    King Rappy  (Boss)
                    Alys        (Boss)
                    Profound Darkness (Final Boss)
```
